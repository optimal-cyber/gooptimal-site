SKU 01 · Federal

Optimal Federal.
FedRAMP-shaped AI inference.

A drop-in, OpenAI-compatible inference plane that lives inside the authorization boundary. Your defense product calls our endpoint; we route to a FedRAMP-authorized model in gov-cloud; we prove it to your assessor.

SWEEP · CHANNEL · PROVE
// Sweep 01 / 03

Recon before the assessor does.

Scan customer codebases and runtime workloads for unauthorized LLM calls. OpenAI, Anthropic, Cohere, Gemini keys. SDK imports. Prompt strings hitting non-authorized endpoints. Output: a boundary-violation report and a migration plan, before the DIBCAC or 3PAO assessor finds it first.

// Channel 02 / 03

Route through the authorized boundary.

OpenAI SDK-compatible API. Single endpoint. A compliance-floor router sends each request to the right authorized model on Bedrock GovCloud, Vertex Assured Workloads, or Azure OpenAI Gov. Zero-persistence by design. Prompts and responses live in Lambda RAM and nowhere else. Only metadata is stored.

// Prove 03 / 03

Evidence emits as a side effect.

Machine-readable FedRAMP 20x KSI evidence emits continuously from gateway traffic. Your SSP, your DIBCAC package, your 3PAO console all read from a live feed instead of a quarterly screenshot binder.
