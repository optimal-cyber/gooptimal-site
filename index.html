01 / CONTAINER HARDENING

"Our containers don't stay hardened."

Image scanning catches build-time issues. Production drifts. STIG and CIS gaps cause findings the team can never quite close. Hardening reads like a one-time project, not a state.

→ How we engage: Optimal applies STIG and CIS baselines pre-runtime, generates fix PRs with concrete Dockerfile and Helm patches, and detects drift continuously.

02 / COMPLIANCE EVIDENCE

"Audit time is a quarterly fire drill."

SOC 2, PCI, HIPAA evidence is screenshots and spreadsheets, collected the week before the auditor arrives. Engineering writes compliance content instead of shipping.

→ How we engage: Evidence emits as a product feature. SOC 2, PCI DSS v4.0.1, HIPAA, ISO 27001:2022, pulled live from operational state. Auditor reads a live feed, not a binder.

03 / FINDINGS VS FIXES

"Our scanner ships findings, not fixes."

Vulnerability scanners are good at "you have a problem." They're bad at "here's the patch." Tickets stack up because triage isn't fix.

→ How we engage: Optimal opens fix PRs in your Git for the findings it surfaces. Engineers review and merge. They don't research.

04 / SaaS VS SELF-HOST

"We can't put cloud security in someone else's cloud."

The leading CNAPPs are SaaS-only. Customer data plane sits inside the vendor's cloud account. Procurement balks. Sovereignty teams balk. So does the auditor.

→ How we engage: Optimal is self-hosted. Helm chart, one command, your cluster. Optimal never holds your credentials. Optimal never holds your data.
